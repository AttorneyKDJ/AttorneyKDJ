About Me /ስለ እኔ /Waayee Koo

Kidane Deyasa Jiru
Attorney at Law, Legal Consultant & Academic Researcher

Welcome to my professional legal practice. I am a dedicated attorney, consultant, and academic researcher committed to providing high-integrity, evidence-based legal solutions. My career bridges the gap between deep academic legal analysis and practical, real-world advocacy in the courtroom and consulting office.

With extensive experience rooted in the Ethiopian legal framework, my practice focuses on delivering precise counsel in civil litigation, criminal defense, complex labor disputes, and business law issues. Alongside my private consultancy, I maintain a proud affiliation with the academic community, where I contribute to the legal field through scholarly research, rigorous content analysis, and the development of legal aid project initiatives 

In my philosophy, the law is not just a set of rules, but a vital tool for ensuring justice, protecting human rights, and fostering social cohesion. Whether conducting academic research or representing a client in a complex civil or criminal dispute, my practice is anchored on three unwavering pillars: absolute confidentiality, deep empirical accuracy, and accessible legal aid.

In a constantly evolving legal landscape, I remain committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity and accountability, providing you with the peace of mind you deserve.
